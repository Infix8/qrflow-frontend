About E-Cell SMEC

DREAM DESIGN DELIVERY

The Entrepreneurship Cell (E-Cell) at St. Martin's Engineering College is a student-run body dedicated to fostering the spirit of entrepreneurship among students. We aim to create a vibrant ecosystem that encourages innovation, creativity, and the development of new ventures.

Our mission is to provide a platform for aspiring entrepreneurs to learn, network, and grow. We organize workshops, speaker sessions, competitions, and mentorship programs to equip students with the necessary skills and knowledge to turn their ideas into successful businesses.

By collaborating with industry leaders, successful entrepreneurs, and prestigious institutions like IIT Bombay, we strive to provide our students with the best possible resources and opportunities.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate in this event? add

This event is open to all students from any college or university who are interested in entrepreneurship. Whether you're a budding entrepreneur or just curious, you are welcome.

Is it a team event or an individual event? add

You can participate individually. The registration fee is per person.

What do I get for the registration fee of ₹650? add

The fee covers your participation in all sessions, activities, and the pitching competition. It also includes the full list of incentives like the certificate from E-Cell IIT Bombay, a startup kit, free e-summit passes, and much more. Please check the 'Incentives' section for a complete list.

Do I need a business idea to participate? add

Not at all! The workshop is designed to help you with ideation techniques. You will learn how to generate and refine business ideas during the sessions. The event is perfect for those at the very beginning of their entrepreneurial journey.
