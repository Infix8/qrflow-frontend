Technical Requirements

To participate in the digital workshop, you'll need:

  • Stable internet connection (minimum 2 Mbps recommended)
  • Computer, tablet, or smartphone with camera and microphone
  • Updated web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
  • Quiet environment for participation
  • Notebook and pen for taking notes

Access Instructions

How to Access Your Workshop

  1. Check your email for workshop access details
  2. Click the provided link at the scheduled time
  3. Join the workshop using your registered name
  4. Ensure your camera and microphone are working
  5. Participate actively in all sessions

Troubleshooting

If you experience technical difficulties:

  • Check your internet connection
  • Refresh your browser or try a different browser
  • Ensure your device meets the technical requirements
